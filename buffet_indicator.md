# Buffett Indicator Dashboard

This page visualizes the output of `stocks.download_us_gdp_and_market_cap`, which lives at `calculated_data/us_gdp_and_market_cap.csv`. The dataset contains monthly estimates of:

* `gdp_constant_2017_usd` — U.S. real GDP expressed in chained 2017 dollars.
* `market_cap_2017_usd` — Total U.S. equity market capitalization, deflated to 2017 dollars.
* `buffet_indicator` — The ratio of market capitalization to GDP.

Use this dashboard to compare the growth of GDP vs. market cap and to track the Buffett indicator through time.
