The [Buffett indicator](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buffett_indicator).

* `gdp_constant_2017_usd` — U.S. real GDP expressed in chained 2017 dollars, we use an estimate of the current quarter to be able to estimate recent Buffett indicator values
* `market_cap_2017_usd` — Total U.S. equity market capitalization, deflated to 2017 dollars, estimated using VTI ETF 
* `buffet_indicator` — The ratio of market capitalization to GDP.
